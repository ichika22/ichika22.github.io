THE ESSENCE

Why we expect an edge, long-term.

Not indicators. Not secret sauce. Just three principles that survive 20 years of data — and they are built into every EA in this series.

01

Win rate lies.
What matters is expectancy

You can build a game with an 83% win rate that loses money every single day. Whether a system makes money is decided by expectancy — what remains, on average, after all the wins and all the losses. This series only trades setups whose expectancy was positive across decades of historical data. One trade means nothing; the sum of hundreds does.

02

An edge is found by testing,
not by feeling.20+ years of backtests

Spreads, commissions, slippage — every FX trade starts underwater by the cost. A setup that stays profitable after paying all of it can only be found by testing rules against decades of past data. Every EA here runs exclusively on rules that survived verification since 2003.

03

The hardest part is
sticking to the rules.Machines don't panic

Prospect theory says the human brain is wired to take profits too early and hold losses too long. And in a deep drawdown, everyone wants to abandon the system — right at the bottom. A machine follows the rules 24 hours a day without feeling any of it. That is the real value of automated trading.
